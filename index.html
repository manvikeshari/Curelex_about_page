About Us

Curelex is a next-generation hybrid e-clinic model delivering super-speciality healthcare, telemedicine, diagnostics, and pharmacy support to rural and semi-urban communities — all through a zero-invasive OPD-based system. At Curelex, patients visit nearby e-clinics where trained doctors perform basic checkups before connecting them to super-specialists via secure telemedicine. We partner with diagnostic labs, local pharmacies, and hospitals for seamless sample collection, quick medicine access, and referrals when needed. Our mission is to make expert medical care accessible, safe, and affordable — bridging the rural-urban healthcare gap through technology and trust.

Curelex — Telemedicine that feels like offline care.
